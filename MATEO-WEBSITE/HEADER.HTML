Our goal at Heart & Soul Co. is straightforward: to raise awareness of heart health issues and the grave dangers of heart attacks. We think that life is saved by awareness. Our goal is to enable every Filipino to identify early warning signals, make better decisions, and take care of their hearts—one heartbeat at a time—by means of education, empathy, and practical advice.
